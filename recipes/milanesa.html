Milanesas

Description

Milanesas are a common argentinian food, which consists of breaded beef that can be fried or baked.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of beef thin cut
  • 200g of panko
  • 1 egg
  • 1 spoonful of milk
  • Salt, Oregano and other spices to your liking

Steps

  1. Mix the egg, milk and condiments until it is well integrated.
  2. Pass each slice of beef through the panko, the mix and the panko again.
  3. Repeat with every slice of beef.
  4. Fry until golden them or bake them in the oven at 220ÂºC for 25 minutes.
