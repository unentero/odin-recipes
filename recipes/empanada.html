Empanadas

Description

The empanada is a south-american food, which consists of condimented ground beef inside a dough pocket that can be fried or baked.

Ingredients

  • Premade empanada dough (sold by the dozen)
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 hard boiled eggs
  • Olives to your liking
  • Salt, Oregano and other spices to your liking

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and bell peppers in small cubes and Sauté them in a pan.
  2. Add your ground beef and condiments.
  3. Turn off the stove and add the chopped olives and hard boiled eggs to de mix.
  4. Fill each dough pocket with a spoonful of filling
  5. Fry them or bake them in the oven at 200ºC for 20 minutes.
